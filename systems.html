Our advanced security system technologies make your security and safety worry-free! With our lines of ePIR®, eVoxBox®, eVoxCon®, and eNaviGuard® products, we strive to be ahead of the competition, and to give YOU peace of mind.

ePIR® is the upgraded version of current PIR motion sensing technology and directs the future development of motion detection applications. By using passive infrared-based motion detection technology (PIR), ePIR® achieved considerable improvements in both sensitivity and stability, effectively lowering the probability of false alarms triggered by pets, bugs, fans, etc..

As the world’s first pure digital motion sensing technology, ePIR® is widely employed in all kinds of commercial, residential, industrial, security, & safety projects around the globe..
